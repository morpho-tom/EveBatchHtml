Smart Helmet using YOLO

In the modern era, road safety has become a critical concern due to the alarming increase in traffic accidents, particularly those involving two-wheeler riders. Helmets are an essential protective gear designed to safeguard riders from severe head injuries, but non-compliance with helmet usage remains a major issue. To address this, we propose an AI-Powered Helmet Detection System using the advanced deep learning model, YOLO (You Only Look Once), designed for real-time object detection. This system integrates artificial intelligence with computer vision to automatically detect whether two-wheeler riders are wearing helmets. By leveraging YOLO, known for its speed and accuracy in object detection, the system ensures reliable performance under various conditions, such as different lighting, angles, and scenarios. This innovation eliminates the need for manual intervention in helmet enforcement, thereby reducing human error and workload for law enforcement agencies.
